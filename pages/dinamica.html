Dynamic Programming (DP) Model

Dynamic Programming (DP) is a method used to solve complex problems by breaking them down into simpler subproblems. It is particularly useful in optimization, where problems can be divided into overlapping subproblems with optimal substructure. DP is widely applied in fields like resource allocation, shortest path problems, and inventory management to find efficient solutions by solving smaller, manageable pieces.
