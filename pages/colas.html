Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) Model

The Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) model calculates the optimal inventory level a company should order to minimize storage and ordering costs. By finding the balance between demand, ordering costs, and carrying costs, it optimizes the efficiency of inventory management. This approach helps businesses reduce excess stock while ensuring they have enough inventory to meet customer needs, ultimately leading to improved cash flow and operational efficiency.
