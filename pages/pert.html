Pert Calculator Instructions

Please enter the predecessors for each activity in uppercase letters, separated by spaces.

For the duration of the activity, only positive values are allowed. You may enter decimals using a period.

If you wish to indicate that an activity has "Start" as a predecessor, insert a " " character.
